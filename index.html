About Me

I am a game technical artist with 10+ years of experience in Unity. I have expertise in designing and implementing the ‘Unity layer’ which links the art and animation of the game artists to the functionality of the programmers’ underlying codebase. I also have experience creating animations, particle effects, and custom shaders when necessary. My drive to solve problems in a thoughtful, elegant way has resulted in maintaining a high degree of polish and quality across all the games I have worked on.

Skills

  • Expertise in Unity engine and Unity UI
  • Ability to write clean and performant C# code when necessary
  • Experience of different architectural approaches to building a game in Unity
  • Experience working closely with programmers and the ability to communicate technical information to them remotely
  • Ability to mentor game artists on technical issues
